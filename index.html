Our story

Three ingredients, one obsession

Flour, water, and salt — that's the whole recipe. What takes years to get right is the timing: when to fold, when to rest, when the oven is finally hot enough. Every loaf here starts with a starter we've fed daily since we opened.

We bake in small batches, sell out most afternoons, and wouldn't have it any other way.
